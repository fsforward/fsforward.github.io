What is this website?

What is the point of creating something if no one can See, Listen or Use it?

That was the thought that ran through my mind as I sat in front of my computer, staring at the countless of projects I had made over the past few years. I had poured my time and soul into these project's, but it was all for nothing if no one could See, Listen, or Use it. So, with a deep breath, I decided to put my work online. I uploaded my work to Instagram & YouTube and waited for the reactions.

At first, there was silence. I began to doubt my decision and wondered if I had made a mistake. But then, a notification popped up on my screen. Someone had liked my project. And then another, and another. Soon, I was receiving messages from people all over the world, thanking me for my work and telling me how much they love my work & that i should never stop.

I realized then that the point of creating something was not just about the final product, but about the impact it could have on others. By sharing my work online, I was able to reach a wider audience and make a difference in the lives of people I would never have met otherwise. It was a humbling and gratifying experience, and one I will always remember.
