You won't understand

I want to die, I don't want to live.

I've heard it countless times: "You can do it, you're just lazy. Stop with your excuses." But if I say that I am not able to do something, then I mean it. So no, it's not just an excuse, and I'm not lazy. I simply can't.

If you were to look into the future, you might see a "standard," or what people would call a "normal" life. A job, maybe a family. But I see hell. If my future life is about having a job, why not end it now?

I want to be alone, no, I need to be alone. I am scared of people, and I don't want to be around them. So a job? Working with people? No.

I have autism. I wake up every day with a lot of stress, and it only builds up throughout the day. If I am not alone in my room, in my house, I will reach my crisis point, resulting in a shutdown.

I am constantly stressed and scared of everything, from doing something wrong to people around me talking about me or hating me.

My mom knows I have autism but sees me as a normal person. "Nothing is wrong with you," she says. "Nothing is wrong with me," maybe not in others' eyes, but that's the thing: no one understands me.

I either want to die or just stay alone in my room. Otherwise, I have too much stress. I hate living.

I don't see the point in living anymore.

The only reason I'm still here is because of my mom. Otherwise, I would've been gone already.

Every day, I stay up late at night, hoping my brain rots away, eventually ending it all. Sadly, I am still here.

I don't want to go outside. I don't want to take the train. I don't want to be a programmer. But people don't understand me and expect me to have a "normal" life, a job for income, because that is the oh-so "most important." So, you're telling me it's better to literally die from the inside than to live "happy"?

The reason I've typed this is because I can't talk about it. I don't have friends, or at least they cannot help me in any way. Not family that can help me in any way. No, they would probably send me to therapy, as if that helps?! That would only make it worse, but again, you would probably not understand the reason of WHY.

My life is already destroyed. It started falling apart since I was like 7, and it only went downhill from there.

I am literally empty. Empty of energy, empty of feeling. I'm existing, not living.
