fsPNGTuber

A program that switches between two images based on your microphone activity for PNG Tubers. Unlike similar tools limited to platforms like Discord or OBS, this offers an overlay positioned on your screen, making it 'compatible' with screen recorders like oCam or Bandicam.

  • Easter egg PNG's.
