Overview

It started raining bomb’s from out the sky!

Dodge the bombs to stay alive. The more bomb’s you dodge the more score you gain.

Core gameplay

  1. Moving: Move to dodge bombs to stay alive.
  2. Score: Keep track of your score, when you die your goal is to break your old score.
